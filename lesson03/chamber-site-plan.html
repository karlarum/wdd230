Niceville Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is "Niceville Chamber of Commerce" because it efficiently explains the purpose of the site, a chamber of commerce, and provides the location, Niceville.

Site Purpose

The Niceville Chamber of Commerce website content will include upcoming events, popular attractions, existing members, the ability to sign up to become a member, and contact information. This website is designed to promote local businesses and provides resources for potential members.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact the chamber's board of directors?
  • Are there resources available to help me grow my business?
  • Where can I find updates about local businesses and events?
